About Us

GeeksforGeeks@REC Student chapter was formed in 24th December 2023.The objective of this student chapter is to enhance the student learning in various fields.

Our Objectives

Enhancing Coding Skills

Our student chapter's will enhance coding skills through various means, fostering a collaborative and learning-oriented environment. We will also conduct sessions.

We will conduct various hackathons.

Enhancing Design Skills

We are also going to focus on enhancing the design skills as designing elements or websites can be really helpful in enhancing our overall skills and also we can work towards creating new designs.

We are going to conduct design workshops.

Industry Expert Sessions

Industry Expert Session's will teach the club members regarding the lastest technologies which are required for the current job market as well as the current industry.

We will have expert talks once in a month.

Community Building

Students in our chapter will be able to connect with different mindsets of each and every branch through which we assume that the innovation could be possible.

We will ensure the club meetings every week.

Personal Development

We will make sure that everyone in the student chapter will be having some positive output after each and every monthly session and also they can discuss regarding the doubts with all the leads.

We will conduct sessions.

Monthly Contests

Every month we will be having hackathons as well as challenges. These challenges could help each and everyone to know about the competition.

We are going to provide the cool swags from the official geeksforgeeks.
